Brownies Recipe!

A simple yet tasty brownies recipe!

Ingredients

  • Cocoa
  • Semi sweet chocolate chips
  • Unsalted butter
  • Vegetable oil
  • Granulated white sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Corn Starch

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325 degrees celcius
  2. Grab a bowl, and mix the dry ingredients together.
  3. In a different bowl, mix the wet ingredients together.
  4. Now combine the wet and dry ingredients together by sprinkiling the dry mixture over the wet mixture.
  5. Pour the batter into a metal baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  6. Place the pan into your oven and bake for 40 to 45 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the brownies comes out with only a couple crumbs stuck to it.
  7. Allow the brownies to fully cool down and then slice and enjoy!
